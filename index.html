About Us

MEFood is a fastest growing restaurant discovery website, established in 2020 by Ritik Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah. Initially, it was named as Foodiebay but in 2021, it was renamed as MEFood. It provides not only information related to nearby restaurants but also provides facilities such as online ordering, table reservations and management.The Indian food delivery, and restaurant discovery platform plan to raise Rs 9,375 crore through the newly formed IPO. It comprises a brand new issue of equity shares equal to Rs 9,000...

From swanky upscale restaurants to the cosiest hidden gems serving the most incredible food, MEFood covers it all. Explore menus, and millions of restaurant photos and reviews from users just like you, to find your next great meal.
